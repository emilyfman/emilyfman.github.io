Emily Man

Emily is an investor at Redpoint Ventures focused on growth investing. At Redpoint, she has invested in companies including Ramp, FloQast, Orca Security, and Proper Finance. Prior to Redpoint, Emily was at Point72 Ventures focused on early-stage fintech investing where she backed companies including Mantl, MX, and Brace. Emily earned her BA from Columbia University.
